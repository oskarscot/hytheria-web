Skills unlock permanent bonuses and leaderboard placement.

## Fast leveling tips

- Focus on one profession at a time.
- Craft boosters before long grind sessions.
- Pair mining with dungeon runs to maximize XP.

## Milestones to hit

Aim for level 10 in your primary skill before branching out.
