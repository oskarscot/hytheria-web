Island upgrades determine your buildable area, storage capacity, and unlock new automation options.

## Core upgrades

- **Expansion** increases build limit.
- **Storage** unlocks larger vaults.
- **Energy** powers advanced minions and defenses.

## Recommended order

Start with Expansion, then Storage, and save Energy until you have a steady income stream.
