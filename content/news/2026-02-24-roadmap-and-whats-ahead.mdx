Hey everyone! Things have been a little quiet on our end lately, but that's because we've been heads down working on some exciting stuff behind the scenes.

We've got a ton of content in the pipeline and wanted to share a rough roadmap of what's coming so you know what to look forward to.

## Update 0.0.11 — Aiming for tomorrow evening!

1. **Essence Extractor** - a brand new tool for your minions
2. **Leaderboard** rebalancing and tracking fixes
3. **Player `/tpa` command** finally!
4. **Vote reward** bug fixes
5. **"Sell All"** option at traders for quick selling

## Update 0.0.12 — First week of March

1. **Mob spawning** getting a full rework
2. **Sprinkler improvements** along with a fresh new UI
3. **Minion UI** polish and updates
4. **Dungeon overhaul** (more details on this one soon - there's a lot to cover)
5. **New sickle tiers** for better harvesting

## Update 0.0.13 — Middle of March

1. **Skill unlocks** - new and properly working unlocks across all skills
2. **Skill and level HUD** so you can track your progress at a glance
3. **Farming collections** to give your grind more purpose
4. **Animal Breeding** integrations
5. **Florist powder** - scatter it to grow vegetation in an area
